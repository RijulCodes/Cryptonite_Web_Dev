Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection. It's flavorful, spicy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer into cubes and marinate with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Chop the bell peppers and onion into chunks.
  3. Skewer the marinated paneer, bell peppers, and onion alternately on the skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers on medium heat or in an oven until golden brown, turning occasionally for even cooking.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or sauce of your choice.
