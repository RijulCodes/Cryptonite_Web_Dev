Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer cubes, grilled or baked to perfection. It's served with mint chutney and makes for an excellent vegetarian dish!

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon Gram flour (optional, for a thicker marinade)
  • 1 Bell pepper, chopped into chunks
  • 1 Onion, chopped into chunks
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • Skewers for grilling

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Add the paneer cubes, bell peppers, and onions. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for best results.
  2. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) or heat up your grill. Skewer the paneer, bell peppers, and onion alternately on skewers.
  3. Grill the skewers on medium heat or bake them in the oven for 15–20 minutes until the paneer is golden brown and slightly charred at the edges.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney or your favorite dip.
